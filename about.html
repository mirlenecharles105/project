WHY ARE WE HERE?

Hello! There are thousands of homeless shelters in the United States and very little funding and materials to give out to incoming and residing tenants. We take all things from pencils to furniture that we ship out to different places around the United States to either be given to shelters, salvation armies or through neighborhood give aways to people in need. Some of these items are also brought to a few thrift stores that sell these donated items and use the money for funding to build more shelters.
